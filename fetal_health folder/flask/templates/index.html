Predict and monitor Fetal Health.



Predicting and monitoring fetal Fetal Health is an ML project that involves using machine learning algorithms to analyse and predict the patterns in the fetal health. The dataset contains data from cardiotocograms (CTGs). CTGs describe the results of ultrassounds; the readings show the fetal heart rate, uterine contractions among other indicators of fetal health. They are essential and simple in providing data about the current health of the fetus as well as might be the first indicator of fetal distress. The data collected for the patients was classified by doctors into 3 categories: Normal, Suspect and Pathological
